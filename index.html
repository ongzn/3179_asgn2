Smoking Mortality Rate: Global Perspectives

Smoking Mortality Rate Across the World in 2019

The highest smoking mortality rates are observed in Southeast Asia, parts of Eastern Europe, and several African nations.

This suggests that these regions may have higher smoking prevalence or less effective tobacco control measures.

Gender Distribution of Smoking Mortality

In Malaysia, males overwhelmingly account for the majority of smoking-related deaths.

The persistent gender gap suggests that public health interventions targeting male smoking behavior are critical in reducing smoking-related mortality.

Trend of Smoking Mortality by Year

The overall trend shows a relatively steady rate of smoking-related deaths from 1990 to 2019, with slight fluctuations.
